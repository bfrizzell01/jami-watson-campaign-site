About Jami

I’m a proud Township of Langley resident, a mother, and a 27-year Costco employee. As a hard of hearing woman, I’ve experienced firsthand the importance of accessibility, inclusion, and ensuring every voice has the opportunity to be heard. Throughout my career, I’ve learned the value of hard work, teamwork, accountability, and serving people from every walk of life. Through volunteering with Rotary, serving on the Township’s Parks, Recreation and Culture Advisory Committee, and connecting with residents across our community, I’ve developed a deep appreciation for what makes the Township such a special place to call home.

I’m running for Township Council because I believe great communities are built through connection, thoughtful leadership, and a shared sense of responsibility. I want to help create a Township where families can thrive, seniors can age with dignity, businesses can succeed, our natural spaces are protected, and every resident feels they belong.

Together, we can build a future that honours what makes the Township special while embracing the opportunities ahead.
