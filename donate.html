Support a Community-Driven Campaign

This campaign is built by neighbours, volunteers, and people who believe local government should be transparent, thoughtful, and accountable.

I’m not a career politician. I work full-time at Costco, volunteer throughout our community, and have spent the past 15 years learning, listening, and showing up because I believe local government has the power to improve everyday life.

If you’ve followed my journey and believe in the kind of leadership I’m offering, I’d be honoured to have your support.

Every contribution, whether it’s $10 or $100—helps me connect with more residents across the Township of Langley.

WHERE YOUR DONATION GOES

Every dollar is used to support the campaign, including:

  • Lawn signs
  • Printed campaign materials
  • Website and communications
  • Community events
  • Social media and digital outreach
  • Connecting with voters across the Township

I believe in transparency. Your donation supports my campaign and helps share my vision for a Township where people feel they belong, decisions are made thoughtfully, and we leave Langley better than we found it.

THERE ARE MANY WAYS TO HELP

Not everyone is in a position to donate, and that’s okay. You can also support this campaign by:

  • Volunteering your time
  • Displaying a lawn sign
  • Sharing my campaign with friends and neighbours
  • Following and sharing my social media
  • Most importantly, voting on Election Day!

Every act of support, no matter how big or small, helps build a stronger campaign and a stronger community.

Thank you for believing that local government can be thoughtful, transparent, and rooted in service. Together, we can build a Township where everyone has the opportunity to belong.

HOW TO DONATE

IMPORTANT: Before making a donation, please confirm the following:

  • You are a resident of British Columbia.
  • You are a Canadian citizen or permanent resident of Canada.
  • The contribution is from your own funds and is not reimbursed by another person or organization.
  • Your total contributions do not exceed the 2026 Campaign Contribution Limit of $1,429.70.

By donating, you confirm your compliance with these rules as outlined in the Local Elections Campaign Financing Act.
